Created by Joxean Koret after discovering the price of OPSWAT Metascan and knowing the real work behind it.

Malice: VirusTotal Wanna Be - Now with 100% more Hipster

Created by blacktop. Malice's mission is to be a free open source version of VirusTotal that anyone can use at any scale from an independent researcher to a fortune 500 company.
